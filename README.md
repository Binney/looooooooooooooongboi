# loooooooooooongboi

Engine for looongboi. Powered by Raspberry Pi Pico.

A variety of features. Lights, sound and input.

## Setting up

Flash pico with CircuitPython.

Copy the whole of lib/ to pico.

Customise main.py for your use. Examples in example.py.

Copy over main.py to pico root and you're golden.
